About Me

Background & Interests

I am a second-year Electrical Engineering student at RWTH Aachen University, one of Europe's leading technical universities. My studies focus on Biomedical Systems Engineering, where I work with tools like MATLAB/Simulink and develop software in C++.

Alongside my studies, I work as a Working Student at devolo Solutions GmbH in Aachen, where I conduct hardware qualification tests, support technical purchasing, and carry out electrical and mechanical modifications on devices. This role lets me apply academic knowledge in a real industrial environment every week.

Prior to university, I gained early research exposure through an internship at SNAP GmbH in Bochum's R&D department, where I analysed EEG data and trained machine learning algorithms — an experience that sparked my interest in the intersection of electronics and life sciences.

Away from engineering, I am a proud member of VfL Bochum and stay active through endurance sports and judo. I also pursue photography and investing as personal interests.
